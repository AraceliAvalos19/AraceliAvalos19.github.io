White space and clean design

Tifanny & Co.

tifanny.com

The right amount of white space makes a website look “clean.” While a clean design is crucial to communicate a clear message, it doesn’t just mean less content.

The amount of white space in Tifanny’s web page makes this website looks clean. The white space makes it easy to focus on the beautiful products. It has no visual competence, allows us to admire the jewels and easily find the information we need.

Repetition

IN-N-OUT BURGER

in-n-out.com

Repetition is achieved by repeating an element more than once throughout a design.

IN this web page the principle of repetition gives the feeling that the customer has control, since in a simple and original way all navigation options are shown and it is easy to choose what the user is looking for. With this design, we can easily find a job, a location or choose a hamburger.

Contrast

MARS INCORPORATED

mms.com

Through contrast designers are able to highlight certain elements they want to emphasize, creating a focal point for an audience and a visual hierarchy.

m & m´s image always handles colors. On this website, the company manages the classic brown color that distinguishes it, the design is very simple, with simple shapes and contrasting colors with brown. It is easy to read, easy to find what you are looking for.
